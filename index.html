About Forays'24

About Forays'24

"Mathematics is the music of reason" - So goes the new age adage and FORAYS, the annual mathematics festival at IIT Madras is testimony to it. FORAYS is a manifestation of mathematical erudition of the highest order; a test of intellect and imagination; a provocative game of mathematical techniques - from guessing to counting! FORAYS is indeed a tryst with maths.

The Department of Mathematics has been successful in bringing together the brightest young minds every year for a mathematical fiesta. Brimming with vigour and innovation, FORAYS 2024 aspires to take this success to new heights.

So join us for two days filled with mathematical adventures in the form of fun events, quizzes, guest lectures, and even more !
